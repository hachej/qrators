# Beeper - All Your Chats in One App

## Overview
Beeper is a unified messaging application that consolidates multiple popular chat platforms into a single interface, enabling users to manage all their conversations from one inbox.

## Key Features

**Supported Messaging Platforms:**
The app integrates with 12+ messaging services including WhatsApp, Instagram, Telegram, Google Messages, Google Chat, Messenger, Signal, LinkedIn, X, Discord, and Slack.

**Core Capabilities:**

1. **Secure Communications** - "Beeper supports on-device connections for most chat apps" with messages traveling directly from your device to the network for maximum privacy.

2. **Multi-Device Support** - Available across macOS (Apple silicon and Intel), iOS (iPhone and iPad), Android, Windows, ChromeOS, and Linux with seamless message synchronization.

3. **Advanced Features** - "Manage your inbox like a pro and never miss a message again with reminders, scheduling, and more" through the Beeper Plus subscription tier.

## Pricing & Availability
Beeper operates on a freemium model with optional paid subscription. Users can download and use the app at no cost across all supported devices.

## Company Information
Beeper is owned by Automattic Inc. and operates as an independent application with no affiliation with the chat platforms it integrates (Apple, Google, Meta, etc.).
